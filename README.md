# OKADA Foundation LBG

Empowering the Street. Fueling the Future.

OKADA Foundation is a nonprofit organization focused on improving the livelihoods, safety, and economic opportunities of motorcycle riders across Africa.

## Our Focus Areas

- Vocational Skills Development
- Financial Literacy & Entrepreneurship
- Road Safety & Professional Training
- Legal Support & Rider Advocacy
- Rider Welfare & Emergency Support

## Geographic Focus

- Ghana
- Liberia
- Sierra Leone

## Programs

The Foundation delivers programs through partnerships with training institutions, community organizations, and industry stakeholders to support workforce development and mobility sector transformation.
